### 📋 內容腳本（Content Script）

**中文概念：**
POV 反轉敘事——「被低估 → 震撼反轉 → 全場矚目」三段式情緒弧線，利用視角切換製造強烈反差感，觀眾代入旁觀者視角。

**English Concept:**
POV reversal narrative — "underestimated → shocking transformation → commanding presence" three-act emotional arc. Viewer adopts bystander POV for maximum vicarious impact.

---

### 🎬 分鏡畫面（Shot List）

**中文分鏡：**
  **S1 — 建立低調（0–3s）**
  手持輕微抖動的中景，主體低頭走入，燈光偏暗，服裝不起眼——刻意讓觀眾低估
  **S2 — 轉折時刻（3–6s）**
  主體慢慢抬頭/轉身，鏡頭開始從手持轉為穩定Steadicam跟拍，燈光開始升亮
  **S3 — 大反轉（6–10s）**
  燈光全亮，鏡頭快速拉開至全身廣角，展示完整造型——停止移動，讓造型自己說話
  **S4 — 特寫強化（9–12s）**
  緩慢推進至半身近景，主體肩膀後展，直視鏡頭，眼神從溫柔轉為強勢，捕捉眼神變化的精確幀
  **S5 — 離場收尾（12–15s）**
  主體轉身走遠，鏡頭不追（靜止），只看背影與裙擺/外套在光線中的輪廓——餘韻感

**English Shot List:**
  [Establish Underdog]
  Slightly unsteady handheld medium shot, subject enters with head down, dim lighting, understated outfit — deliberately underwhelming
  [Pivot Moment]
  Subject slowly raises head / turns, camera transitions from handheld to smooth Steadicam, lighting begins to brighten
  [Power Reveal]
  Full lighting on, camera snaps to wide full-body to reveal complete look — camera stops completely, let the outfit do the talking
  [Authority Close-Up]
  Slow push to medium-close, shoulders roll back, subject locks eyes with camera — capture the exact frame where expression shifts from soft to powerful
  [Exit Shot]
  Subject turns and walks away, camera holds static — only see silhouette of back and garment hem/coat against light — lasting impression

---

### ✂️ 轉場方式（Transitions）

**中文轉場：**
  **S1→S2 → 燈光溶解（Light Dissolve）**
  利用燈光從暗到亮的過程做0.5秒溶接，光線變化本身即是轉場
  **S2→S3 → 動作硬切（Action Cut）**
  主體完成轉身動作的最後一幀硬切至廣角全身，利用動作完成點做切入點
  **S3→S4 → 靜止推進（Static Creep）**
  不切，直接在廣角上做緩慢電動推進（不抖動），3秒內從全身推到半身近景
  **S4→S5 → 轉身遮幕（Turn Wipe）**
  主體轉身動作帶動衣物/頭髮遮住鏡頭的瞬間，切入靜止背影遠景

**English Transitions:**
  [S1→S2] 燈光溶解（Light Dissolve）
  0.5s dissolve driven by lighting change — the light transition IS the transition, no abrupt cut
  [S2→S3] 動作硬切（Action Cut）
  Hard cut at the exact completion frame of the turn — action endpoint is the cut point, creates satisfying "snap" to wide shot
  [S3→S4] 靜止推進（Static Creep）
  No cut — continuous slow motorized push-in from wide to medium-close over 3 seconds, zero shake
  [S4→S5] 轉身遮幕（Turn Wipe）
  Subject turn causes garment or hair to momentarily fill frame — cut to static far back-shot at that opaque frame
